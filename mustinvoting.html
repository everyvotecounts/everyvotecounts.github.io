MUST IN VOTING

Essential Steps to follow when voting

MUST

  1. Know your Precint Number. Be aware of your precinct number and voting center location.
  2. Prepare a List of Candidates. Create a “kodigo” or list of candidates you intend to vote for to prevent errors.
  3. verify Information. Fact-check candidates to make an informed choice, rather than following popular opinion.
  4. Avoid Campaigning. Refrain from campaigning or discussing political views at the polling site.
  5. Take your Time. Vote carefully to avoid mistakes.
  6. Confirm your Vote Before leaving, make sure your vote has been counted correctly.
