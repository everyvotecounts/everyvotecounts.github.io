Not Registered Yet? Here's How to Get Started

  • Step 1. Verify the voter registration requirements in your area.
  • Step 2. Gather the necessary documents (e.g., valid ID, proof of residence).
  • Step 3. Complete your voter registration form.
  • Step 4. Submit your form at the nearest registration center or online, if available.
Don’t wait—ensure you’re ready to make your vote count in the next election!
